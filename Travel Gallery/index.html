This is my personal gallery for pictures of my life and travels around the world.

2010 Mt. Carmel: This was my first international trip. I went to Israel for a mission trip with my church and one of the places we went to was Mt. Carmel. This picture shows the team members from our church we went to.
2010 Caesarea: This was also in Israel and one of the first places we went to. If I remember correctly, this place was previously used as a stage area and when I went back here in 2017 they were reconstructing the front stage.
2012 Kilo 41: This was my second mission trip and this was in Indonesia. This picture was taken at Kilo 41 where we were doing ministry with the kids. We were in the jungle during the time and saw a dancing monkey.
2017 Sea of Galilee: This was my second time in Israel and my second time riding the boat in the Sea of Galilee. This picture was taken from the view of my hotel at sunrise.
2017 Abraham's Tent: Camel Ride- Also my second time riding a camel and I got my second time I got my camel license. I am licensed to ride a camel lol.
2018 Swayambhnath Monkey Temple: Monkey stole my water bottle- This was in Nepal and a monkey jumped on my bag and snatched my water bottle. Apparently, the tour guide told us to put away our water bottles, but I was sleeping.
2018 Himalayas: We got to see the Himalayas. We had to wake up really early to go to the view point because a lot of people go there. It was a really amazing sight and I got to accomplish one of my dreams.
2018 Seoul Tower View: After Nepal, we stayed in Korea for awhile and we went around. One of the places we stopped at was the Seoul Tower and the view was amazing.
2019 Sunset in Athens, Greece: This was my sixth mission trip and I went without my parents. Greece was not what I expected, but it was an amazing experience.
2019 Meteora Monastery in Greece: There were a lot of nice view points in Greece and a lot of ancient sites to visit. This place just had an amazing view of Meteora and we were really high up.
2019 Mycenae in Greece: This was a cave and it had a really huge domed roof. There were also bats inside.
2019 Neapolis in Greece: This was a short stop and we got to see the ocean and city of Neapolis.
2019 Colosseum in Rome at night: I went to the Colesseum quite often because our hotel was right by it. There was less people at night..
2019 Colosseum in Rome in the morning: This is the view of the Colesseum from the inside in the morning. The lines were so long and it was very hot this day.
2019 Vatican City in Rome: Vatican City was unbelievably crowded. We could barely move once we went inside and we were stuck inside for awhile.
2019 Disneyland in LA: I did a lot of travelling this summer and after about 2 weeks after I came back from Rome, my mom and I went to LA. The first places we went was Disneyland.
2019 Universal Studios in LA: It was my first time going to Universal Studios. The studio tour was great and the rides were very thrilling.
2019 Patbingsu at Koreatown in LA: This is a Korean dessert and it was really good. The ones they sell in Hawaii can't compare to this.
2019 Melon and Mango Patbingsu at Koreatown in LA: Another style of Patbingsu we ate.
2019 Grandfather's 90th Birthday Celebration: Family Picture (without dad) in LA: The real reason we went to LA was for my grandfather's 90th birthday party. I got to meet many family members and had a fun experience.
2019 Cruise to Mexico: So my aunt wanted my mom and I to go on a cruise with my grandparents (which was not part of the original plan), but I was very excited and it was a very great experience.
2019 Cruise to Mexico: View from deck: The sunrise and sunset views were amazing. Although if were not close to land or when the sun was completely up, the only thing you could see was ocean.
2019 Cruise to Mexico: Stop at Catalina Island- We stopped by Catalina Island and I didn't even know this island existed. You could go paragliding here.
2019 Cruise to Mexico: Tour in Mexico- We finally landed in Mexico and we went on a tour. We tried fish tacos and go to go shopping in Mexico. It was very cheap in general, but you could easily bargain to lower the price even more.
